Cheese Pizza

Description

Classic Cheese Pizza is a simple yet delicious dish featuring a crispy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce and generous amounts of melted mozzarella cheese. Baked until bubbly and golden, it’s a favorite comfort food enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Transfer dough to a baking tray or pizza stone.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top and add herbs.
  6. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  7. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.
